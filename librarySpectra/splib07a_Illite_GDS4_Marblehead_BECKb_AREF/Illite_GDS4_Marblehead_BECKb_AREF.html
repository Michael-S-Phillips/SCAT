XRD_ANALYSIS:

Analysis by Norma Vergo indicates that the sample is illite/smectite: 80% illite layers, R3 ordered, with trace amounts of feldspar. The less than 2µm separate is illite + illite/smectite.

END_XRD_ANALYSIS.
